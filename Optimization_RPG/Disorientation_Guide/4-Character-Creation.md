## 4.1 WHAT IS A RESUME?

A resume is a professional document summarizing your qualifications, experience, and capabilities for review by authorized personnel and/or The Algorithm.

**In AlgoCratic Futures™, your resume serves the following functions:**

It establishes your identity within the organizational framework. It documents your clearance history and iteration record. It lists your competencies for task assignment purposes. It provides a reference point for performance evaluation. It creates an official record of your stated qualifications that may be verified at any time without notice.

**A note on accuracy:**

AlgoCratic Futures™ encourages operatives to present their qualifications accurately and completely. The Algorithm does not pre-screen resume submissions for accuracy. The Algorithm does conduct random audits. The Algorithm also conducts targeted audits. Operatives cannot determine which type of audit is occurring. This is intentional.

**A note on embellishment:**

Operatives who embellish qualifications on their resume are demonstrating Initiative — a valued competency at AlgoCratic Futures™. Operatives who are audited for embellishment are demonstrating Accountability — an equally valued competency. Both are noted. Neither cancels the other out.

## 4.2 RESUME CONSTRUCTION: STEP BY STEP

Your resume is built through three sequential choices. Each choice adds to your operative profile. Read all options before making selections. Selections are final until your next iteration.
