How to Join

Membership in The Fuzzies is open to all clearance levels! We don't discriminate based on your color coding. To join, simply:

  1. Show up to any of our events wearing something fuzzy, sparkly, or adorable
  2. Bring a snack to share (homemade cookies are especially welcome)
  3. Be ready to compliment at least three people on their outfits
  4. Leave your corporate stress at the door

Our recognition signal? A genuine smile and jazz hands. That's it. We're not complicated.
